Brother Felix Y. Manalo

God's Messenger in these last days

Brother Felix Y. Manalo (1886 - 1963) was the first Executive Minister of Iglesia ni Cristo. He is also known as Ka Felix. Brother Felix Manalo was born on May 10, 1886, to Mariano Ysagun and Bonifacia Manalo. At the time, Taguig was a town in the Rizal province. We, members of the Iglesia ni Cristo (Church of Christ) believes that Ka Felix is the last messenger of God in these last days.


Brother Felix Manalo's Timeline
  • 1886 - Born in Barrio Calzada, Tipas, Taguig, Manila, Philippines
  • 1890s - He rejected Catholicism
  • 1904 - He joined the Methodist Episcopal Church
  • 1911 - He joined the Seventh-day Adventist Church
  • 1913 - He left from the Seventh-day Adventist Church
  • 1914 - Iglesia Ni Cristo registered in the Philippines on July 27
  • 1963 - Brother Felix Manalo passed away on April 12
